Refresh Your Soul

Nestled deep within a lush tropical forest, Sumber Pemandian Kemado offers an escape from the hustle of modern life. Our pools are fed continuously by a natural underground spring, ensuring the water remains crystal clear, cool, and refreshing year-round.

Surrounded by ancient trees and the sounds of chirping birds, it is the perfect place to reconnect with nature.
